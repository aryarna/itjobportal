Privacy Policy

Welcome to IT JOB. Your privacy is our top priority. Since our platform is designed for pure information sharing, we have a very straightforward privacy approach.

1. No Data Collection

Unlike other job portals, we DO NOT require you to create an account, login, or provide any personal information. We do not collect your name, email, phone number, or resume.

2. No Tracking

We do not use invasive tracking cookies to monitor your personal identity. You can browse all IT jobs and categories anonymously.

3. Official Applications

All "Apply Now" buttons on our site are direct links to the official career websites of the respective companies. Your application process happens entirely on the employer's official platform, not ours.

4. Search Optimization

Our advanced fuzzy search and location filters work locally on the data we provide. We do not store your search history on our servers.

5. Transparent Information

We only aggregate publicly available job information to help you find the best IT opportunities in one place. We are a bridge between you and your next big career move.

By using this site, you agree to browse responsibly. Safe job hunting!
